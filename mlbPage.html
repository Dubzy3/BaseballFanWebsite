What is the MLB

The MLB stands for Major League Baseball. It is the most historic professional sports team in the United States and the oldest. It was founded in 1876 in Cincinnati Ohio. The MLB consists of two leagues the National League, NL, and the American League, AL. Each league is split into 3 divisions: East, Central, and West. These divisions consist of 5 teams, for a total of 30 teams in the MLB.
